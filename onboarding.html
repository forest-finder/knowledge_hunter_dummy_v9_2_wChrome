Getting Started

Knowledge Hunter helps you capture, organize, and retrieve knowledge from anywhere on the web. Here's how to get started:

  1. Capture Knowledge
    Use the extension to save entire pages, selected text, links, or images.
  2. Organize Your Captures
    Create collections to categorize and organize your knowledge.
  3. Search & Retrieve
    Quickly find what you need with powerful search capabilities.
  4. Sync Across Devices
    Sign in to access your knowledge anywhere (optional).
